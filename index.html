Alistair Westbrook UNL MECH. ENGINEERING '29
Hello! I'm a freshman Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln with a strong passion for the automotive sector.

My real passion is at the intersection of hardware and software. I'm a builder at heart, whether I'm prototyping physical components as a member of the Baja SAE team or building a full-stack web application from scratch.

This "hands-on" approach is what led my hackathon team to win 2nd place for Terraflo Analytics, and it's what drove me to build Differdle.com, a calculus-based web game.
